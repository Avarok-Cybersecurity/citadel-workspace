# Getting Started

Welcome to Citadel! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## Creating Your Account

1. Enter a username and password
2. Configure your security settings (optional)
3. Join or create a workspace

## Navigating the Interface

- **Sidebar**: Browse offices and rooms
- **TopBar**: Access your profile and settings
- **Content Area**: View markdown content and chat

## Your First Steps

1. Visit the Welcome office
2. Introduce yourself in General chat
3. Explore the different rooms

## Security Features

Citadel uses end-to-end encryption to protect your communications. Your messages are encrypted before they leave your device.
